1. Introduction: What this codelab teaches. An overview of how to use the Play Billing Library to sell one-time products.
2. What are One-Time Products?: An explanation of one-time products (both consumable and non-consumable) as defined by Google Play Billing.
3. Setup Play Billing Library and Connect to Google Play: Configure the BillingClient and establish the connection to Google Play.
4. Query for Available Products: Implement the logic to fetch product details from the Play Console.
5. Initiate the Purchase Flow: Launch the Google Play purchase screen for a specific product.
6. Handle Purchase Results: Implement the PurchasesUpdatedListener to receive updates from the billing flow.
7. Acknowledge a Non-Consumable Purchase: Process a successful purchase for a one-time entitlement by acknowledging it.
8. Consume a Consumable Purchase: Process a successful purchase for a consumable item by consuming it.
9. Best Practices for BillingResult Response Codes: Understand how to handle different billingResult response codes for robust error handling.
